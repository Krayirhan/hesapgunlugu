# Privacy Policy for Finance Tracker

**Last Updated: December 25, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Finance Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Local Data Storage
All your financial data is stored **locally on your device only**:
- Transaction records (income/expense)
- Category information
- Scheduled payments
- Savings goals
- App preferences and settings

### 2.2 Data We DO NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect any personal information
- We do NOT access your contacts
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT send data to external servers
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
- We do NOT share data with third parties

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for:
- Displaying your financial transactions
- Calculating statistics and reports
- Providing scheduled payment reminders
- Generating export files (PDF/CSV)

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Local Security
- Optional PIN code protection
- Biometric authentication (fingerprint/face)
- Screenshot protection (optional)
- All data encrypted in SQLite database

### 4.2 Cloud Backup (Optional)
If you choose to use Google Drive backup:
- Encrypted backup files stored in your Google Drive
- You control backup creation and deletion
- We cannot access your Google Drive files

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or transfer your data to anyone. All data remains on your device.

## 6. Third-Party Services

The app may use:
- **Google Play Services**: For app distribution and updates
- **Google Drive API**: Only if you enable cloud backup (optional)

These services have their own privacy policies.

## 7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- Export all data (PDF/CSV format)
- Delete all data from app settings
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data

## 8. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date.
